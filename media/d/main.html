Celebration of Deepavali

Introduction to Deepavali

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is a major festival celebrated
by millions in India and around the world. Known as the
"Festival of Lights," it symbolizes the victory of light over
darkness and good over evil. The festival spans five days and
is rich in cultural and religious significance, with roots in
Hinduism, Sikhism, and Jainism.
